Services

Application Development

Our specialty is development of web-based applications for your business. We have extensive experience creating complete business solutions for a wide variety of industries.

At Madriska, we use the Ruby on Rails framework to develop web applications, allowing us to spend less time writing “boilerplate” code and more time focusing on your business’s needs.

Ruby on Rails Consulting

Whether your needs are large or small, Madriska can help. From large-scale systems architecture consulting down to the smallest projects, we are available for consulting on any aspect of web application engineering: system architecture, code review, Rails training, analysis and review of existing projects, and more.

Brad Ediger, founder of Madriska Media Group, has written a book on Rails application development for O’Reilly Media. Order it from Amazon: Advanced Rails. The book has also been translated into Japanese (実践 Rails) and Chinese (Rails高级编程).

Hosting and Maintenance

We offer a variety of web hosting packages for anything from small “online business card” sites to large, high-traffic web applications. Prices start at $15/month; contact us for details.

Prefer your own server? We can also provide systems administration services for Linux-based servers.
